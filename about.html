Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Excellence

Tareq Cleaning Solutions was founded in 2011 with a simple mission: to deliver commercial cleaning services that genuinely make a difference. What started as a small, family-run operation has grown into one of Australia's most trusted cleaning companies.

Over the years, we've built our reputation on three core principles: consistency, quality, and reliability. Every member of our team is carefully selected, thoroughly trained, and deeply committed to exceeding expectations.

We believe that a clean workspace isn't just about appearance — it's about creating healthier, safer and more productive environments for everyone. That's why we use eco-friendly products and constantly innovate our approach to deliver the best possible results.

Our Values

What Drives Us Every Day

Our core values shape everything we do — from how we hire and train our team to how we serve each and every client.

Integrity

We operate with honesty, transparency and accountability. Our clients trust us with their spaces, and we take that responsibility seriously.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

From humble beginnings to an industry leader — follow our journey of growth, innovation and commitment to excellence.

2011

The Beginning

Tareq Cleaning Solutions was founded with just 3 team members and a vision to deliver the highest standard of commercial cleaning in Sydney.

2013

First Major Contract

Secured our first major corporate client — a 10-storey office building in Sydney CBD. This marked the start of rapid growth.

2015

Green Cleaning Initiative

Transitioned to 100% eco-friendly cleaning products. Became one of the first commercial cleaners in Australia to go fully green.

2017

Healthcare Expansion

Launched our specialised medical and healthcare cleaning division, obtaining all required hospital-grade certifications.

2019

Interstate Growth

Expanded operations to Melbourne and Brisbane, growing our team to 30+ dedicated professionals serving clients across three states.

2021

500+ Client Milestone

Reached 500 active clients and received our 10th industry award for excellence in commercial cleaning services.

2023

Technology Integration

Implemented smart cleaning technology, real-time quality tracking and a digital client portal for seamless communication.

2025

Gold Coast & Beyond

Expanded to the Gold Coast region with 50+ team members and plans for nationwide coverage by 2027.
