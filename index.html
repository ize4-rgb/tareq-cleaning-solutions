FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

We've compiled answers to the most common questions about our cleaning services. If you can't find what you're looking for, don't hesitate to reach out.

Contact Us

Will I have the same cleaner every time?

Yes, absolutely. Barring holidays or illness, you will have the same dedicated cleaner every time. This ensures consistency and builds trust between our team and yours. Your cleaner will know exactly what your space needs.

Do you lock clients into contracts?

No, we don't believe in lock-in contracts. We earn your business through consistent quality service. You're free to adjust or cancel at any time — we're confident you'll want to stay.

Do you clean during the day or at night?

We offer flexible scheduling to suit your business. Whether you need cleaning during business hours, after hours, on nights or weekends, we'll work around your schedule to minimise disruption.

What products do you use?

We use premium, eco-friendly cleaning products that are effective yet safe for people and the environment. For specialised settings like healthcare, we use hospital-grade disinfectants that meet all regulatory standards.

Are your cleaners insured and background checked?

Yes. All our cleaning professionals are fully insured, police-checked and hold relevant accreditations. We take security seriously and only hire trustworthy, experienced professionals.

How quickly can you start?

In most cases, we can begin within 48 hours of your initial enquiry. After a brief assessment of your space, we'll put together a custom cleaning plan and get started right away.
